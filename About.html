OUR HISTORY


Excellent Nursery and Primary School started as a private mixed school with classes; baby to Primary Seven respectively but with out a UNEB center for P.L.E examinations.

By 2023, Excellent Nursery and Primary School had acquired all the basic requirements for the licence and registration by the Minstry of Education ans Sports, Registration No PPS/E/121.

We are a Christian founded school and we produce well nurtured and God fearing pupils. We offer a holistic UNEB Curriculum alongside practical skill development anf Computer studies.

We cater for children in day-care, nursery and primary level. We offer a nurturing, engaging and conducive environment for a child's learning process. Our dedicated and experienced staff are keen on the development of your child's mind and character in a healthy and fun way.
